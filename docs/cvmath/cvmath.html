  • Incompressibe fluid $\implies\newline \nabla\cdot \vec{u}=0 \implies \frac{\partial u_k}{\partial x_k}=0 \newline\implies \sigma_{ij}=\mu(\frac{\partial u_i}{\partial x_j}+ \frac{\partial u_j}{\partial x_i})$
