Pressure · Deformation · Amplification · Angular Displacement

① Pressure Unit Conversion
1 mmHg = ρHg · g · h
       = 13600 × 9.807 × 0.001
       = 133.3 Pa
P in Pa = P [mmHg] × 133.3
② Capsule Deflection
δ = P · Aeff / k

k = E·t³ / R²(1−ν²) · Cgeom
δ [m] : axial tip displacement of capsule
③ Lever Amplification
Δs = δ · (L1 / a)

M = L1 / a    [mechanical advantage]
Typical M ≈ 5–15×; converts µm deflection → mm arc
④ Rack & Pinion Conversion
Δθ = Δs / rp

Δθ = δ · L1 / (a · rp)
r_p = pinion pitch radius [m]
⑤ Dial Calibration
θ(P) = (3π/2) · P / Pmax

Sdial = π/200 ≈ 0.9° / mmHg
270° span over 300 mmHg full scale
Full Sensitivity
S = Aeff · L1 / (k · a · rp)

MAP ≈ Pd + ⅓(Ps − Pd)
